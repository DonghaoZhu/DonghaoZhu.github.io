How to pronounce my name?

  • My first name, Dong hao, is quite easy to pronouce since I have tested it on many of my friends. They seem to make it very successfully. The Chinese characters are 东浩 (simplified) or 東浩 (traditional), which stands for being generous and grateful.
  • My last name, Zhu (朱), is pronounced much the same way as Chu or True. This is the surname of an ancient Chinese emperor (Zhu Yuanzhang) whose empire lasts around 300 years (1368 - 1644). But now there are millions of people with the surname "Zhu" in China, so I should have nothing directly to do with him.
